About / Legal

Publisher: KodeXpo.

This extension processes Flickr page data to draft, review, and apply metadata only for the user's own Flickr photos and other content they own or control. Depending on the selected provider, photo text and image data may be sent to OpenAI, DeepSeek, or a local Ollama instance to generate draft metadata. The extension handles authentication information such as Flickr OAuth tokens and account identifiers, website content such as Flickr page titles, descriptions, tags, and image data, and locally stored draft metadata and settings. It stores this information locally in Chrome on the user's device. It does not sell user data or use it for advertising.
